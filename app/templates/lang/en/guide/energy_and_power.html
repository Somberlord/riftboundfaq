Energy & Power

There are 2 Resources in Riftbound: Energy & Power

  • Energy is represented by Numerical Values, and is color agnostic :
  • Power, which is represented by the Domain symbols and is color specific :

The simplest and most common way to generate these resources is with your Runes. You a Rune to generate Energy, and you Recycle a Rune to generate Power.

Both resources can also be created by other abilities and effects, such as exhausting a Seal for Power.

Whenever you generate either resource, they go into a virtual pool that can be used to pay for Costs of the appropriate type. That pool doesn't empty until the end of the turn, and at the end of the draw phase.

Note that some cards and effects can cost both Energy and Power.
